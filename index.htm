Protect yourself and your love one's in COVID19

Wash your hands often


Wash your hands with soap frequently, especially when you are in a public area after blowing your nose, coughing or sneezing, with soap and water at least 20 seconds.

Use a hand sanitizer which contains at least 60 percent alcohol when soap and water are not readily available. Cover and rub together all surfaces of your hands until they feel dry.

Wear a mask when you are around with others


The mask is designed to protect other people.

In public settings and when around people who don't live in your household,
everyone should wear a mask, particularly when other social distancing measures are hard to maintain.

Practice social distancing


Keep 6 feet distance if the ohter household member is sick'

Set a distance about 6 feet between yourself and people who don't live with your household, Remember that some people without symptoms may be able to spread virus.

Cover coughs and sneezes


When you cough or sneeze, either cover your mouth and nose with tissue or use the inside of your elbow and do not spit.

Wash your hands immediately for at least 20 seconds with soap and water.
